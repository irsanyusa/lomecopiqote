Last night, an athlete was revealed on The Masked Singer! And from what we gathered, we could have another one disguised as the Deer.

From the first look, you can tell the Deer is a big guy. But just how big? And who could it be? Let’s take a look at the clues and who we think might be behind the Deer mask:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CuRGR0HN5T0

Week 1 Clues

Height: 6’3″

Strength: Longevity

Pre-Show Clues: First, we saw a stuffed horse then a bunch of gauze pads.

Episode 1 Clues: The Deer says he is competitive, while holding a trophy. He said he’s doing this because it’s hard for people to get past who he is and what he’s known for. The Deer then says, “In the Wild Wild West, you learn how to get up and get back in the saddle.” He also warns “Ravens beware” and says he loves horses?

Episode 1 Song: “Thunder” by Imagine Dragons

Our Week 1 Prediction

Terry Bradshaw (Honorable Mentions: John Elway, Clay Matthews, Tim Tebow, Brock Lesnar)

There are strong implications that whoever the Deer is has connections to a horse. But isn’t a straight-up Broncos/Colts connection too obvious? We’re thinking it might be, but it’s not out of the question. Here’s something to consider: in a video released before The Masked Singer premiered, it said the contestants have a combined total of 4 Super Bowl titles. With Antonio Brown already unmasked, that means there’s another NFL player in the mix. If it is the Deer, Terry Bradshaw makes the most sense, because he’s won four Super Bowls. It’s unlikely they’d have three football players on the show! The Deer mentions Ravens in his clues, possibly because the Ravens and Steelers are rival teams and Terry played for the Steelers. The Deer has a little Southern drawl too, which Terry is known for.

But this one is a little more up in the air. Most of the clues have been steering things in pretty much one direction. We’d also say the Deer could be John Elway, Clay Matthews, Tim Tebow, or professional wrestler Brock Lesnar.

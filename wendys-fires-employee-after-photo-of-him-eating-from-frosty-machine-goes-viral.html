Wendys Fires Employee Over Photo of Him Eating From Frosty Machine

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Here’s a simple formula for getting your 15 minutes of viral fame:

1) Work at a popular fast food restaurant.

2) Have a co-worker snap a photo of you doing something gross on the job, preferably involving one or more food items.

3) Sit back and let the Internet do the rest of the work, spreading that photo across the web.

(MORE: Wendy’s Introduces Lobster and Caviar Burgers)

That’s what a Wendy’s employee did this week, following in the footsteps of a Taco Bell employee who caused an uproar earlier this month after a photo surfaced of him licking a stack of taco shells. The latest fast food prankster — a copycat, perhaps? — posed crouching beneath a Frosty machine, eating the soft serve directly from the nozzle.

Naturally, the Taco Bell employee was fired soon after the photo made its rounds, and it seems the Wendy’s employee has realized a similar fate.

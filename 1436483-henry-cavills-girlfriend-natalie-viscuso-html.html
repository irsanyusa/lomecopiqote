Natalie Viscuso is an actress, movie producer, director, and Hollywood executive. When she was 15, Viscuso appeared on an episode of MTV's series My Super Sweet 16.

The show's 2015 season featured her celebrating her 16th birthday. The synopsis for the episode indicates that she is the daughter of a wealthy man and could have everything she wanted.

She worked with Robert Townsend, the CEO of Robert Townsend Film Life, Universal Studios, and the NFL. From 2012 to 2014, she was in the customer experience department.

Later, she briefly worked in the TV Lit Department of The Gersh Agency. After that, she joined ITV Studios, working with Tomorrow ITV Studios and Scripted Television for two years and five months.

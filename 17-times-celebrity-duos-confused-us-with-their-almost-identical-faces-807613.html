17 Times Celebrity Duos Confused Us With Their Almost Identical Faces

Publish date: 2024-06-29

According to a 2015 study, there is a one in 135 chance that you may have the same looks and quirks as another person somewhere in the world. Even Hollywood stars and other celebrities can’t escape from this fact.

Bright Side is always astounded with A-listers having uncanny resemblances with one another. That’s why we compiled a few photos of celebrity pairs who can make you ask yourself which is which.

1. Harry Styles and Charlie Heaton

2. Cole Sprouse and Brooklyn Beckham

3. Leighton Meester and Lily-Rose Depp

4. Angelina Jolie and Kate Siegel

5. Rob Lowe and Ian Somerhalder

6. Gerard Butler and Clive Owen

7. Nina Dobrev and Victoria Justice

8. Zooey Deschanel and Katy Perry

The iconic fringe and baby doll eyes might give it away, but Zooey and Katy are not twin sisters. Their personalities and quirks are so different that Zooey recalled, “The only similarity that we have is that we look a little bit alike.”

9. Sebastian Stan and Daniel Brühl

10. Emma Watson and Kiernan Shipka

11. Haley Lu Richardson and Zoey Deutch

12. Jeremy Renner and Nathan Fillion

13. John Mulaney and Grant Gustin

14. Jessica Chastain and Bryce Dallas Howard

From their strawberry blonde locks, megawatt smiles, and green eyes, it’s hard to tell Jessica and Bryce apart. The two look like they were pulled straight out of The Parent Trap, as Jessica would go as far as saying, “We grabbed each other’s hands, and we said, ’Let’s go to the mirror.’ And we went to the mirror, and we looked at each other... and we were like, ’Yeah, we look exactly alike.’”

15. Emily Blunt and Sarah Paulson

16. Jesse Eisenberg and Michael Cera

17. Fredrik Robertsson and Jared Leto

There was quite a confusion during the recent Met Gala when the paparazzi seemingly caught two Jared Letos on camera. However, it was not the case as it was fashion influencer and model Fredrik Robertsson who opted for the silver geometric ensemble and not Jared, who chose to don a simple suit.

Which duo made you look twice at their photos? Which of them fooled you with their almost identical features?

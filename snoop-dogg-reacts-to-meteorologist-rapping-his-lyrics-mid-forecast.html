Snoop Dogg Reacts to Meteorologist Rapping His Lyrics Mid-Forecast

Publish date: 2024-06-23

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Johnny Nunez/Getty Images for The Recording Academy, Snoop Dogg via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Snoop Dogg is a name that has become synonymous with hip-hop music and culture over the past few decades. His unique style, laid-back attitude, and lyrical prowess have earned him a legion of fans around the world. However, it’s not just music fans who have taken notice of Snoop’s talent, apparently, even weathermen are impressed!

Snoop Dogg Praises Meteorologist for Rapping The Lyrics of His First Single

Recently, Adam Krueger, a meteorologist at CW39 in Houston, decided to take on a challenge from one of his fans on TikTok. The challenge? To incorporate the lyrics to Snoop Dogg’s first solo single, “Who Am I?(What’s My Name?),” into his weather forecast.

Krueger’s performance was not only impressive but also caught the attention of Snoop Dogg himself, who reposted the clip on his Instagram page. It’s not surprising that Snoop appreciated Krueger’s effort, as the rapper has always been famous for his love of wordplay and clever rhymes.

However, Krueger’s use of song lyrics in his weather reports is not a new phenomenon. He has previously incorporated lines from songs by Beyoncé, The Notorious B.I.G., and Eminem into his forecasts.

It’s hard to deny that Krueger’s use of Snoop Dogg‘s lyrics in his weather report was a stroke of genius. It’s a fun and creative way to connect with viewers and inject some personality into an otherwise routine task.

Snoop Dogg Jokingly Mocks Fellow Rappers

Snoop Dogg has always been known for his playful sense of humor and his ability to make people laugh. The rapper often shares memes and jokes with his millions of fans on Instagram, and his latest post has left his followers in stitches.

Snoop posted a meme that targeted four fellow rappers – Drake, DJ Khaled, NAV, and French Montana. The meme featured a collage of four pictures of the rappers, along with the caption “When you have the same mom but different dads.”

The meme is a lighthearted way of poking fun at the fact that all four rappers share similar physical features, despite coming from different backgrounds and having different musical styles. It’s also a nod to the fact that many people have commented on the resemblance between the rappers over the years.

Snoop Dogg‘s latest meme is just another example of his unique sense of humor and his ability to connect with his fans. While some may see the post as controversial or offensive, most people will simply appreciate it for what it is, a lighthearted joke from one of the most iconic rappers of all time.

During his interview on My Africa Mr ‘Change your parade’ revealed he’s set to release a new album soon.

He said: "I feel like this is my first year as an entertainer because this is six years down the line and there so much I’ve learnt, there’s so much I’ve picked up on the way, there’s so much experiences I have to share".

“I feel like the album I’m about to put out is going to be like my debut for a lot of people even though it’s my sophomore album”, he added.

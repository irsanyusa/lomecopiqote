America’s Got Talent is airing its seventh episode of auditions this Tuesday. This is unlike the other years when they only aired six episodes of auditions. The reason behind this maybe due to the Covid-19 pandemic where they are trying to buy time to tape the other episodes due to a halt in shooting. Let’s see the ‘AGT’ lineup!

‘AGT’ Week 7 Lineup.

1. Alex Hooper

Alex Hooper is a stand-up comedian, actor and professional roaster. He’s the only contestant to ever get a “yes” from Simon Cowell and a “no” from the rest of the judges. His AGT performance has over 50 million total online views.

Alex released his debut comedy album Hugs. Drugs. Pugs. It hit #1 on Amazon and #3 on iTunes Best Selling Comedy Records in 2018.

We will have to wait till this Tuesday to find out if he finds luck in Season 15.

2. The Lightwave Theatre Company

Lightwave Theatre is a Romanian independent visual theatre company that specializes in human-size puppet shows for adults. Lightwave has been performing in countries like in Great Britain, Croatia, Germany, Turkey, Bulgaria which includes three theatre plays: “Reflection”, “Loverphysics” and, most recent, “It’s me, Annabel” since 2013.

The group from Romania owned the stage with a heart-wrenching story about a girl and her dog. This show is performed to “Carry You” by Ruelle. Check out their performance below for Week 7 on ‘AGT’.

3. Voce Nova

It was February 2018 in New York City that classically trained singers Natalia Tsvi and Gilad Pazis decided to decided to join forces when they found themselves at a house concert of opera hits sharing the page passion for music.

VOCE NOVA’s musical performances brings together the luxury of opera and the excitement of contemporary and classic pop music. They have over 40 years of combined on-stage experience singing varying genres and languages.

4. John Sevier Austin

John Sevier Austin, from North Carolina, also known as “Backwords Dude”, has been able to speak backwards perfectly since he was five years. John Sevier Austin credits his ability to talk backwards to Asperger’s syndrome.

He showcased his incredible skill to the people of North Carolina, along with Right This Minute, Good Mythical Morning, Woody and Wilcox, John Boy and Billy, The Gong Show, and ‘The Wizard of Odd TV‘, leaving each and every one stunned.

5. John Hastings

Over the last ten years John Hastings has appeared at every major comedy festival in the world including Edinburgh (2012-2017), Melbourne (2015-2016) and Just for Laughs Montreal.

He has told jokes on BBC Comedy Central, CTV Australian Comedy Channel, and CBC The Comedy Network.  He is also the COCA Comedian of the Year, a Martin Sims Award Nominee, an Amused Moose Comedy Award winner.

6. Chicken Scratch Sam

Chicken Scratch Sam is a comedian from Los Angeles. Sam Brilhart works as a bartender during his free time.

7. Kameron Ross

Kameron Ross is a country music singer from Dallas. He used quarantine to learn how to play the guitar. He is a big endorser for the gay community and is into fitness.

8. The Ninja Twins

Nick and James Aragon – better known as The Ninja Twins. They have performed in So You Think You Can Dance, where they made the world laugh with their audition. They also appeared on Jay Leno’s Tonight Show.

9. Siena Uremovic

The 17 year-old contemporary dancer has won dance competitions in her native Australia. The young performer also has appeared on local tv shows down under. She has over 45K followers on Instagram. 

10. Jefferson Davis High Band

‘Marching Band’ a video of the Jefferson Davis High Band group performs went viral because it’s an all black team led by a funky white guy. 

11. Sheldon Riley

Singer Sheldon Riley competed on The Voice Australia twice. The first time, in 2018, he finished in third place on Team Boy George. The second time, in 2019, he came back as an all-star wearing a beaded mask, which he also dons for his AGT audition. In 2019, he competed on Team Delta Goodrem and was eliminated in the semi-final.

We are excited to see this ‘AGT’ lineup this Tuesday on NBC!

Captain America star is married!

Publish date: 2024-07-12

The ‘Captain America’ star tied the knot with Sheletta Chapital, who is the mother of his two sons, in a very low key ceremony consisting of approximately 150 guests, and was held on a beach.

The pair have known each for most of their lives, and here is what the actor had to say about the his first encounter with his bride during a 2014 appearance on the “Queen Latifah Show".

“It was funny. I was coming from a very bad school and going to a very good school, and when I walked into second grade it was Dr. Seuss Day, and we had the little hot plate out and the teacher was making green eggs and ham. So I walk in, and I looked and I see this little girl with these ratty pigtails and skinny legs, and I was like WOW!”

Thats It! Sofa Vergara Walks Off Americas Got Talent After Howie Mandels Joke

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Sofía Vergara abruptly walked offstage because of a joke one of the America’s Got Talent judges made. The incident sparked quite a reaction among fans. In this article, we delve into the details of the story and explain what exactly made Vergara react this way.

Sofía Vergara recently got divorced.

Sofía Vergara and Joe Manganiello’s relationship seemed to be perfect, and their sudden divorce stunned fans. The couple tied the knot in November 2015, but after 7 years of marriage, they decided to part ways. Vergara and Manganiello announced their divorce but left many questions unanswered, so rumors started spreading. Some say it happened because of different attitudes toward life.

Howie Mandel has been joking about Vergara’s private life.

Over the course of the show’s 18th season, Howie Mandel has made 2 remarks about Vergara’s single status. The first one was during a contestant’s performance onstage, and the second joke was told during a lie detector segment as Vergara was answering questions from the judges.

He told an inappropriate joke.

Mandel asked her if she was interested in anyone in the audience. Vergara couldn’t put up with it, and she shouted, “That’s it,” and quickly got up from her seat.

Fans were quick to react.

Mandel faced immediate backlash for his jokes. He said, “I was helping Sofía! And then online, people said it was too soon and it was tasteless. It’s not too soon! It’s too soon when she’s still married. Last season was too soon. She approved of the joke. She thought it was funny.” And Vergara, in fact, did think so.

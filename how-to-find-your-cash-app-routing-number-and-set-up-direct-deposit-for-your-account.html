How to find your Cash App routing number and set up direct deposit for your account

Publish date: 2024-06-30

The Cash App , by Square , is a peer-to-peer payment app, like Venmo , that allows you to pay friends and family directly, without having to exchange cash or even be in the same place. It also allows you to keep track of who owes you money and what for.

While the app was originally created for this simple purpose, it has since expanded, and allowed you to do things like get a Cash App debit card so you can treat your Cash App balance the same way you would treat your checking account balance.

Taking this capability even further, it is now possible to treat your Cash App like a checking account, by having your paycheck directly deposited into it. You can do this by finding your routing and account numbers associated with your account, and giving them to your employer.

How to find your Cash App routing number

2. Go to the "My Cash" tab by selecting the dollar symbol in the bottom-left corner.

3. Tap the "Cash" button with the green dollar sign next to it.

4. Scroll down to the "Direct Deposit" section and tap "Get Account Number."

5. On the pop-up screen, tap "Enable Account."

6. Tap "Copy Account Details" under your account info.

7. Select "Copy Routing Number" to copy it directly to your clipboard and paste it into an email or message to your employer.
